Our Home Planet

What is Earth?

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. It is about 4.5 billion years old and is home to millions of species, including humans.

Structure of the Earth

The Earth is made up of three main layers:

  • Crust – The outermost layer where we live
  • Mantle – A thick layer beneath the crust
  • Core – The hot, dense center of the Earth

Why is Earth Special? Duwyane

Earth has air to breathe, water to drink, and a suitable temperature for life. Its atmosphere protects living organisms from harmful radiation.
